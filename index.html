How to Use Notes

The notes feature is used to save your important tasks and provides options to add, delete, update, and search. However, please note that your data is stored locally on your mobile device, so LL is not responsible for any data loss or removal in the Notes section.

(I) Add Note

  • Step 1: Click on "Note".
  • Step 2: Tap the add note button at the bottom right corner.
  • Step 3: Enter a title and description.
  • Step 4: Click on the save button (top right corner).

(II) Update Note

  • Step 1: Click on the editable note.
  • Step 2: Edit the title and description.
  • Step 3: Click the save button at the bottom right corner.

(III) Delete Note

  • Step 1: Click on the editable note.
  • Step 2: Click on the delete button at the top right corner.

This process allows you to create new notes, and options for deletion, updating, and searching are also available. Your data is secure as it is stored in local storage.
